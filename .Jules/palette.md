
## 2024-05-19 - Actionable Empty States
**Learning:** Text-only empty states (e.g., "No history yet.") miss a critical opportunity to onboard or re-engage users when dynamic data is absent, leading to dead-ends in user flows.
**Action:** Always implement visually distinct empty states that include a helpful icon, a clear explanatory message, and an actionable path forward (like a button linking to another relevant part of the app) to encourage immediate engagement.
