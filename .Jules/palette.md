## 2024-05-24 - Actionable Empty States for Dynamic Content
**Learning:** Empty states for dynamic content (like user history or sessions) are missed opportunities if they only say "No history". They often lead to dead-ends. Providing a visually appealing empty state with an actionable call-to-action (like navigating to a relevant view) significantly encourages continued user engagement and guides them on what to do next.
**Action:** Always implement empty states with an actionable path forward when designing lists or areas that depend on user-generated content.
