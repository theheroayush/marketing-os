## 2024-05-13 - Actionable Empty States
**Learning:** Empty states in user-generated content sections (like History) are often dead ends, displaying only a simple "No content yet" message. This misses an opportunity to guide users and encourage engagement with core app features.
**Action:** Always provide an actionable path forward in empty states (e.g., a CTA button linking to another part of the application) rather than just stating the view is empty.
