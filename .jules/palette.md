## 2025-05-15 - Actionable Empty States
**Learning:** When displaying empty states for dynamic content (like history, recent sessions, or team members), simply stating the view is empty is a dead end. Users need an immediate, actionable path forward to understand the feature's value and encourage engagement.
**Action:** Always include a call-to-action button linking to another part of the application (like "Explore Skills" or "Manage Team") alongside a brief explanation of what will appear in the space.
